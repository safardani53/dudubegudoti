Kourtney Kardashian And Travis Barker Tied The Knot In Las Vegas Chapel

According to TMZ, the pair performed an intimate wedding ceremony at a Las Vegas chapel in the middle of the night. The pair was in Sin City to attend the Grammy Awards. They arrived at the place where the two wedded only hours after attending the spectacular celebration.

According to TMZ, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star and Blink-182 drummer refused to allow visitors to snap photos at the location. To keep the occasion discreet, the two had hired a photographer and security. According to TMZ, the couple insisted on having an Elvis Presley lookalike conduct their wedding. The couple allegedly arrived at the chapel with a marriage license and presented it to the venue’s owner, who was also there at the time. According to reports, this will not be the couple’s first event to commemorate their commitment. According to TMZ, “several” more events will have significantly greater “fanfare.”

When did Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker get engaged?

In October, the 46-year-old proposed to Kourtney Kardashian. The drummer proposed the beachfront The Rosewood hotel in Montecito, California.

According to a People magazine source:

“The hotel is their go-to for quick weekend trips.” They often stay at the hotel. This is why Travis proposed at that location. They see it as a really precious site.”


They went on:

“It was an extremely beautiful twilight beach proposal.” Travis took Kourtney on a stroll, and you could see she was confused when she spotted all the flowers on the beach. Red and white flowers were arranged in the form of a heart. There were several candles. They both seemed to be in a good mood.”

Despite the fact that the pair has not yet spoken publicly about their relationship, they have traversed the world and swamped social media with PDA-filled photos.

Travis Barker Marriage

Before marrying Barker, the oldest Kardashian-sister had a lengthy romance with Scott Disick. Kourtney Kardashian and Disick have three children, Mason, Penelope, and Reign, who are 12 years old, 9 years old, and 7 years old, respectively. Despite being together for many years, the 42-year-old and the real personality never married.

Travis Barker has two previous marriages before his present partnership. From 2001 to 2002, he was married to Melissa Kennedy. In 2004, he married Shanna Moakler. However, the marriage barely lasted four years. Landon and Alabama, who are 18 and 16 years old, are his siblings.
