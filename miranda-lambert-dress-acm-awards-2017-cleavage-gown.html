If you’ve got it, flaunt it seemed to be the motto of Miranda Lambert‘s, 33, dress as the country crooner arrived on the red carpet at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas on April 2 for the 52nd annual Academy of Country Music Awards, clad in an A-line gown — and the billowing skirt of the dress was balanced out by the severely low neckline which served up an ample dose of cleavage — and Miranda’s figure looked amazing in the frock.

The white and gold printed gown plunged low and then cinched in her waist, enhancing her figure, before giving way to the full skirt. A diamond necklace and matching white clutch added to the glam look. She stuck to the sexy theme as she wore her hair sleek and straight, keeping the focus on her figure — there was no missing her on the red carpet! She was accompanied by her boyfriend, Anderson East, at the show.

Miranda is set to debut her new song, “Tin Man,” off her album The Weight of These Wings at the big show and she ensured she stole the spotlight as she arrived — it definitely set the tone for the night, and you know she’s bound to fit in an outfit change before storming the stage.

From sexy silhouettes to lots of sparkle, all of the looks at the ACM Awards were beyond glam. Also in attendance at the show was Carrie Underwood, Maren Morris, Jessie James Decker, and Nicole Kidman — and you can see all of their glam looks in the gallery above.
