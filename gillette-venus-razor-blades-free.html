How to nab Venus razors for FREE as one woman gifted 34 worth of hair removal goodies

A WOMAN was left stunned when a parcel containing Gillette Venus razors and a £38 eye cream randomly turned up at her front door. 

Yas Firth hadn’t purchased the beauty goodies but quickly realised that they had been sent to her for free by the brands. 

The freebies are thanks to a little-known “online community” called SuperSavvyMe.

Bosses of the organisation say it “helps people get more out of life” by sending free products - sometimes as a gift, other times to review on the SuperSavvy app. 

“If you love testing out new products and sharing honest reviews with friends and family, you’ll love what we have to offer,” they said. 

You can land some freebies by downloading the app and earning points to win products and prizes.

But you don’t typically get to choose what you get freebies - brands will send you packages to review as and when. 

Yas Firth was initially confused when the package arrived at her front door in December. 

But she quickly realised that she’d been sent the bundle as a gift for being part of the SuperSavvy community. 

It included Gillette’s Venus Extra Smooth Snap Razor + Blades and Deluxe Smooth Sensitive Blades three-pack - which would have cost £34 to buy.

The package also included Olay’s £38 Niacinamide 24 + Vitamin E Eye Cream With 99% Pure Niacinamide.

To buy all three products together would have cost Yas just short of £62. 

But thanks to SuperSavvyMe, she was sent them for free. 

She took to Facebook to share the exciting steal, seeking reassurance that the parcel wasn’t sent to her by mistake. 

Others were quick to reassure Yas that the parcel was intended for her and congratulate her on the amazing freebie. 

“Seems you entered a competition and have won,” one explained. “Congratulations. 

“You won’t need to review products or anything like that, but if you do want to post about it, SuperSavvyMe always like being tagged for marketing purposes.”

The Venus Extra Smooth Snap Razor was designed to help women who are on the go. 

It has a mini handle and comes with a travel case so you can take it anywhere.
