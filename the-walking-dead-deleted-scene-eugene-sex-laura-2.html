The Walking Dead star dishes dirt on deleted Eugene sex scene that show bosses axed 'because it was

THE Walking Dead is proof that even the zombie apocalypse isn't enough to stop mankind bonking.

The AMC drama has included a manner of weird and wonderful sex scenes over the years, with the most recent between Negan (Jeffrey Dean Morgan) and Alpha (Samantha Morton), who insisted on doing the deed while wearing a mask of rotten skin.

However, almost as bizarre is the prospect of resident nerd Eugene (Josh McDermitt) enjoying a romp with former saviour Laura (Lindsley Register) - yet that's exactly what writers initially had planned.

Register revealed that a scene between the unlikely pair was axed by producers during the editing stage as they thought it would take away from the seriousness of the action.

Wile appearing on the Talk Dead to Me podcast, Register explained that the hook-up was due to take place while the duo were in Sanctuary as Daryl and Tara tried to break in.

Apparently it was a "kind of nihilistic moment" as the two faced potential death and featured no serious emotional connection whatsoever.
