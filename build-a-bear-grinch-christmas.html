Build-A-Bear is selling The Grinch toys in time for Christmas - and prices start at 19.50

LOOK alive Christmas movie fans because Build-a-Bear has launched a Grinch teddy just in time for the festive season.

Yes despite his (several) failed attempts to steal Christmas, Build-a-Bear Workshop is now selling a grumpy Grinch toy for £19.50.

  • The Grinch Teddy, £19.50 from Build-a-Bear Workshop - buy now

To celebrate the launch of the new animated Grinch movie this adorable teddy is a total must for any festive film fans.

Complete with his bonkers hair do and soft green fur, we promise this super sweet toy is a lot more cuddly than he appears in the movies.

Whether you're planning on giving the gift of the Grinch this Christmas or simply want one for yourself (count us in), customers can either order the teddy online or customise him in stores.

And to really amp up the festive spirit, Build-a-Bear is also selling the character's classic Santa Costume and hat for an extra £14.

  • The Grinch Santa Suit, £14 from Build-a-Bear Workshop - buy now
