Alan Jackson (Country Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Alan Eugene Jackson is an American singer and songwriter. He is known for his blend of traditional honky-tonk sounds and traditional country pop music, as well as for writing many of his own songs with quill. Jackson has recorded 16 studio albums, three hit albums, two Christmas albums and two gospel albums. Jackson is one of the best-selling musical artists of all time, having sold more than 75 million records worldwide, of which 44 million are in the United States alone. He has performed 66 songs on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart; Out of the 66 titles and six standout singles, 38 made it to the top 5 and 35 went first. Of the 15 songs on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart, nine were certified multi-platinum. He is the recipient of two Grammy Awards, 16 CMA awards, and 17 ACM awards, and is nominated for a number of other awards. He is a member of the Grand Ole Opry and was presented to the Georgia Music Hall of Fame in 2001 .
