B&amp;M selling Victoria's Secret perfume for just 7.99

STUCK for a Christmas gift that she will love? Well, we think we've just found the perfect solution that certainly won't break the bank.

Bargain lovers will be amazed to hear that B&M is currently stocking posh designer perfume for just £7.99.

The 250ml Victoria's Secret fragrance mist is currently the store's manager's special and top of the bargains - ringing in at under a tenner.

The refreshing formula is infused with conditioning aloe vera and calming chamomile, which according to the product description will 'leave skin kissed with softness and sensuous scent'.

However, we suggest you make a move fast as stock is set to fly of the shelves and once it's gone, it's gone.
