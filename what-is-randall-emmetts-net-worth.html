What is Randall Emmett's net worth?

Randall Emmett, 52, has produced over 100 movies throughout his career and is best known for his work on the films Silence and The Irishman.

He is the co-founder and serves as chairman of the production company Emmett/ Furla /Oasis Films (EFO Films). 

His films have generated more than $1.2billion at the global box office according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The outlet also estimates that Emmett's net worth stands at $500,000.

How many kids does Randall Emmett have?

Emmett has three kids, London, Rylee, and Ocean Kent.

Actress Ambyr Childers is his ex-wife and the mother of Emmett's two children London and Rylee.

What has Lala Kent said since their breakup?

Kent has claimed that her ex-fiance was dating a 23-year-old the same month she gave birth to their infant daughter, Ocean.

The Vanderpump Rules star broke down in tears during the season reunion as she discussed Emmett's younger mistress and her various grievances against him.
