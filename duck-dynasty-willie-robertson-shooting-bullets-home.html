Duck Dynasty star Willie Robertsons home hit by bullets in drive-by shooting The US Sun

DUCK Dynasty star Willie Robertson and his family were victims of a drive-by shooting when “eight to 10 shots” were fired at his property on Friday.

According to local law enforcement, two of the reality stars’ homes were hit on his West Monroe, Louisiana property but no one was hurt.

The Ouachita Parish Sheriff’s Office responded to a report of someone shooting from a vehicle at approximately 2:30 p.m. on Friday.

Of the nearly dozen shots fired off, one went through the bedroom window where his son John Luke Robertson, 24, lives with wife Mary Kate McEachern and their six-month-old son.

Thankfully, the bullets did not hit anybody and no one was hurt from the wreckage.

Willie, 48, told USA Today: "We were pretty shook up. It looks like they were just spraying bullets across my property.”

The CEO of Duck Commander revealed the shooting happened “in broad daylight.”

No arrests have been made yet, the Ouachita Parish Sheriff’s Office said.

Eyewitnesses who were present at the time observed several gunshots coming from the vehicle - which was described as a white Ford F-250 with brown or beige on the bottom.

Surveillance footage captured a picture of the alleged suspect’s vehicle which matches witnesses' descriptions, KNOE8 first reported.

Those present at the scene also noted the truck had large aftermarket tires and rims.

According to police, the driver appeared to be a young white male in his teens or early 20’s.

The famous outdoorsman revealed that he believes the suspects knew it was his property they sprayed bullets at.

"I'm 100% certain, but I don't know why," he said.

Bullets hit the homes that are behind a secured gate and far away from the main road.

"Nobody was outside at the time, but everybody had been out about 5 minutes before. I had just gone to the store when it happened," Willie added.

Amid the coronavirus pandemic, various members of his family are currently staying at his estate to self-isolate.

Willie and his wife Korie Robertson, 46, were also joined by daughter Sadie Robertson, 22, and her new husband Christian Huff.

Sadie - who was the first-runner-up on Dancing with the Stars in 2014 - married Christian in November 2019.

Ducky Dynasty was a hit on A&E for its 130 episode run from 2012-2017.
