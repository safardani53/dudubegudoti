Golden State Warriors Stars Cute Fam Hollywood Life

Steph Curry is one of the NBA’s best players, but he’s a family man at heart. Steph has been married to wife Ayesha Curry since 2011. They have three precious children together: Riley, Ryan, and Canon. Steph’s family is incredibly supportive of his basketball career with the Golden State Warriors. Ayesha is always in the stands cheering on her man. Ayesha loves to bring the kids to games as well. Riley Curry became a star in her own right after stealing the show at her dad’s press conference in 2015. She has such a great personality — just like her mom and dad. Ryan Curry is equally adorable. The latest Curry to join the family is Canon Curry. Canon actually means ‘officiant of the church’ and ‘young wolf.’ The Curry family is the very definition of #FamilyGoals.
