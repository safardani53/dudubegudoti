KIM Kardashian has shown off a huge new piece of custom bling, leading her fans to believe that’s going through a "midlife" crisis.

Fans took to Reddit to discuss the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum after she posted on Instagram about her new necklace, which features her name spelled out in diamonds.

Kim, 41, showcased the piece as she posed outside with a huge puffer jacket worn off her shoulders.

She showed off her impressive figure in a skintight halter neck tank top and grey leggings.

Her chain was custom-made by fine jeweler Eric Mavachev, also known as Eric Da Jeweler.

Eric has a range of exclusive clients including rappers P Diddy and Snoop Dogg, and Manchester United soccer players Paul Pogba and Marcus Rashford.
