JAZZ'S PAIN

Jazz previously opened up about her weight gain in November 2021.

She confessed at the time that she was "humiliated" and had gained the weight through binge eating on junk food.

The TV personality made the confession in the Season seven trailer for I Am Jazz.

She revealed that she was on a journey to lose weight, confessing to "struggling."

In a video confessional, Jazz said she had been struggling with "mental health issues" which led her to binge eating.

She confessed: "I gained weight and more weight and more weight. And now, almost 100 pounds heavier, here I am today."

While trying to learn how to improve her health, Jazz shared that her "typical breakfast" at the time included "fast food, maybe a doughnut or two, and then maybe two bagels."

Her family watched as she opened up about her eating habits and her struggles with binge eating.

Jazz's mother tried to support her in her weight loss journey but things quickly got tense between them.

"I do experience fat-shaming from my family," the star said at the time.
