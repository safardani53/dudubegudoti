Is That a Nip Slip?

Must we sound the Nicki Minaj Nip Slip alarm once again?

The singer, who is no stranger to showing off her most intimate body parts (see, Minaj, Nicki; shower selfies), has posted two new photos to Instagram, both of which are EXTREME close-ups of her cleavage.

The “Anaconda” artist published the images of herself in a lacy black bra yesterday, leaving so little to the imagination that we’re left to owner: is that a areola peeking out from her left boob?
