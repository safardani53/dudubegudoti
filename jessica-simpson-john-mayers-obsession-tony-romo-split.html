Jessica Simpson claims John Mayers obsession caused Tony Romo to break up with her The

JESSICA Simpson has alleged John Mayer was "obsessed" with her and that his actions led to former boyfriend Tony Romo breaking up with her.

The former pop star has been revealing intimate details of her life with the release of her new memoir, Open Book, and in the book she admits that a therapist made her realise that the singer was never in love with her but instead "obsessed".

"I told my therapist this story a few months back, about John breaking up with me right before I went onstage," she writes before recalling the therapist telling her "that's not love".

She added that the therapist told her: "You know that, right?

"I mean, he never loved you...He was obsessed with you. Love and obsession are so different. One is healthy, one is not."

In the extract, seen by Radar Online, Jessica continued: "She said it so casually, like this was something I should have figured out a long time ago. I felt a dagger, right in my heart again. I was still protective of him."

Elsewhere in the book she revealed that during her two-year romance with football player Tony, John text and Tony saw the message, accusing the singer of seeing John "behind his back".

She insisted that she never "cheated" but that "within that second, he broke up with me right there".

His actions sent her back to John, claiming that she "lost my feeling of agency in my life once again and felt I should just give in and be with John".

"He had promised forever in my parents' backyard. Who was I to argue? I went to John's house quickly. Driving over, I felt like I was in the closing scenes of sweeping, epic love story, and the dark romantic hero had beaten out the star quarterback."

But she shared that when she arrived at his house, it was a "a punch in the gut" and that he was not a changed man".

She said she realised then that she was simply "material" for his songwriting.

Jessica and John had dated between 2006 and 2007, and in her memoir she also alleges that her addictions began with her romance to John, who repeatedly told her "he was obsessed with me, sexually and emotionally".

The pair met at a party in 2005, and he praised her songwriting, and told her how much he loved the track With You.
