Caitlyn Jenner Gets Her First Selfie Stick: Video

Caitlyn Jenner had a better Friday than you: She had some wine and she had a brand new selfie stick to try out, courtesy of … someone!

“Hey! Guess what? Somebody sent me a selfie stick!” the I Am Cait star said in a Facebook video posted Saturday.

Jenner, 66, demonstrated her new toy in a 20-second twirl around her living room: “I love it. I’ve never used one of these before, this is great.”

With selfie stick in one hand and a glass of white wine in the other, Jenner toasted her social media followers ahead of the weekend.
