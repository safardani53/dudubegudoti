Maxwell Trowbridge Gail Jr is an American actor who has appeared in theater, television and film roles. He is known for his role as Detective Stan "Wojo" Wojciehowicz in the sitcom Barney Miller, which earned him two consecutive Primetime Emmy Award nominations for Supporting Role in a comedy series. Gail also won the 2019 Emmy Daytime Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role as Mike Corbin in the drama General Hospital.
