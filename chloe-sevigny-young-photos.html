Photos From Her Young Years to Today Hollywood Life

Chloe Sevigny is a total 90s icon. See her best photos in celebration of her 49th birthday right here.

It-Girl forever! Chloe Sevigny, born November 18, 1974, was just a teen when she first made a name for herself in the 90s indie movie and music scene. She made her film debut in Harmony Korine’s Kids in 1995, and appeared in his cult classic Gummo in 1997. In 1999 she earned an Oscar nomination for Boys Don’t Cry.

Indies like American Psycho (2000), Demonlover (2002); Party Monster, Dogville (both 2003), and The Brown Bunny (2004) would follow. In 2006, Chloe turned to TV with HBO’s Big Love, which earned her a Golden Globe in 2010. Most recently, the star appeared on two seasons of American Horror Story (2013, 2015) and in the films The Dead Don’t Die, Queen & Slim (both 2019), and Bones and All (2022.)

Chloe is a mother of one. The actress welcomed son Vanja with husband Siniša Mačković in May 2020, just two months after they wed.
