6 Best Net Curtains 2020 | The Sun UK

NET CURTAINS allow people to see out of their homes but ensure a restricted view in from the outside.

While occasionally mocked for their very Britishness, the best net curtains are also great for anyone who wants to make their rooms as flooded with natural light as possible, while still retaining their privacy.

Net curtains used to be made from lace, but these days you'll find lots of stylish and contemporary alternatives, like voile, which can be used on their own or layered with fabric curtains.

Net curtains are also a popular choice for allergy sufferers, since they're easy to wash, don't harbour as many mites as classic curtains tend to and allow the windows to be opened while keeping pollen out.

These days, you'll find net or voile curtains in a range of colours, as well as accessorised with tassels or pom-poms.

They're also a cheap way to give any room a quick revamp - you can find net curtains starting from £2.50 a metre.

Most come ready to hang within minutes, whether you have a curtain pole that requires an eyelet or need a slot top finish design.
