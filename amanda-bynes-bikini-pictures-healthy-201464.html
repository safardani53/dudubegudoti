Amanda Bynes Shares Bikini Pictures, Thanks Fans For Birthday Wishes

The next Kim Kardashian? Not likely! But Amanda Bynes did show off some pictures of her bikini body in Twitter on April 5. The recovering star, 28, celebrated her birthday and spring break from the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandise in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico with her parents Lynn and Rick Bynes. 

After photos surfaced of the former child actor in a neon yellow bikini by the resort pool, Bynes posted her own shots on her Twitter account. 

“Thank you everyone for all the birthday wishes,” she captioned one picture of her curvy backside and trim physique. 

The star wore her blonde locks down and wavy with large shades as she smiled, looking happy and healthy in her new photos.
