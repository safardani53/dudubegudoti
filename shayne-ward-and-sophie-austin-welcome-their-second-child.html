Shayne Ward And Sophie Austin Welcome Their Second Child

Shayne Ward And Sophie Austin gave birth to their second kid on June 11 of this year. Despite doctors’ predictions that Austin will have a girl, Ward confirmed on Instagram that they had a boy. He shared a photo of his family with their hands on top of each other. He stated:

“We were pleasantly surprised! We were expecting a baby girl, but instead received a gorgeous baby boy. Sir baby Ward has come, and we are happy and grateful.”

Ward and Austin just announced their son’s name. In an interview with Hello! Magazine, Austin stated:

“There was one name I’d heard that I loved, Reign, and Shayne likes everything medieval.”

All About Sophie Austin

Born on January 5, 1984, she rose to prominence as Lindsey Butterfield in the Hollyoaks serial series from 2013 until 2016. In Hollyoaks, her character was engaged in stories such as hiding the murder of Paul Browning with Cindy Cunningham and Mercedes McQueen, deciding between two brothers Joe Roscoe and Freddie Roscoe being exposed as the Glover Hand murderer, and collaborating with serial killer Silas Blissett.

In the ITV miniseries Tina and Bobby, she portrayed footballer Geoff Hurst’s wife, Judith. Lorne MacFayden, Michelle Keegan, and Patsy Kensit all appeared in major roles in the series, which aired on January 13. She is also known for her appearances in Casualty, Moving On, and Call the Midwife. She is also active on Instagram, where she has over 180,000 followers, and Twitter, where she has 130,000 followers.

Sophie Austin previously married Graeme Rooney in 2015, but the couple divorced the following year. Austin and Shayne Ward started dating in 2016, however it’s unclear where they initially met. In August 2016, the couple revealed that they were expecting their first child. Willow May was born in December of that year, and they got engaged in 2017.

Who exactly is Shayne Ward?

Shayne Ward rose to prominence as Aidan Connor in Coronation Street from 2015 until 2018. He won The X Factor’s second season, and his debut song, That’s My Goal, was released in December 2005. After winning The X Factor, he signed with the Syco record company, and his self-titled first album was released in April 2006. It was an immediate success, and he followed it up with his second album, Breathless, in 2007. Obsession, his third album, was released in 2010 and disappointed in terms of first-week sales. He was dismissed by Syco in 2011 before releasing an album as an independent artist, Closer, in 2015.
