Gwyneth Paltrow flogs 260 bread knife in Goop's Christmas gift guide

HOLLYWOOD star Gwyneth Paltrow is raking in the dough by flogging a bread knife for £260 in her Christmas gift guide.

The movie star, 48, is selling the 10ins knife alongside a £70 orgasm candle and £1,500 grey knitted sweater.

Supermarket Tesco is selling an almost identical bread knife for £6.

On her Goop website, Gwyneth says of the knife: “For slicing bread, of course, but also perfect for tomatoes.”

It guarantees to “take on even the crispiest baguette without the crust exploding all over the counter”.
