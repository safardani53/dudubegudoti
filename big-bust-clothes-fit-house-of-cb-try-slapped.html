I have a big bust - brands don't make clothes that fit but my House of CB try-on slapped, they've go

A CURVY fashionista knows all too well the struggle of shopping with a fuller bust, but she was wowed on a recent shopping trip.

After hearing other style enthusiasts rave about House of CB, she tried on a bunch of the brand's dresses and fell in love — but there was only one problem.

Nubuke (@nubuke) has garnered over 19,000 followers on TikTok, where she shares her adventures around the world.

The midsize fashion lover took to the social media platform to try on outfits found at House of CB in London’s Westfield shopping mall.

“Today I tried on dresses from House of CB because I heard they make clothes for girls with big t*ts,” she said.

WHITE MINI DRESS

Nubuke looked visibly disappointed as the first dress she tried to put on failed to zip up.

The brand’s Clementine white cut-out mini dress is available in cup sizes A to E for $135.

“It’s not fitting me,” she raged while pulling on the side of the dress. 

“I went to their shop in Westfield mall and I had just come back from doing my lashes so you know I was feeling like a babe.

“Then I tried on dress one and I wanted to cry because you know I have a large upper body area.

“And every day the shops don’t want to make clothes for people like me.”

IVORY CORSET DRESS

The fashionista then changed into House of CB’s Tianna ivory ruffle corset dress, which cost $149.

The satin dress has corset hooks for a snatching fit to the waist and an open neckline. 

“Then I tried on dress number two and hmm OK,” Nubuke said.

“I liked what I was seeing, I liked it.”

She danced in the changing room of the store as she looked at herself wearing the mini dress.

STRAPLESS MIDI DRESS

Nubuke looked equally impressed as she tried on House of CB’s Sienna ivory satin strapless corset midi dress.

The $269 dress has an elegant silhouette and is suitable for date nights and glam events, according to the brand.

“I tried on dress three, mhmm,” Nubuke said.

PINK RUCHED DRESS

She tied her braids into a large bun as she continued her shopping spree with House of CB’s Estella soft pink crystallized dress.

The $240 dress is sold in cup sizes A to E as a glamorous style to wear throughout the party season.

“Hmm then dress four,” Nubuke said as she posed confidently.

PLUNGING BLACK MAXI DRESS

She looked pleasantly surprised as she put her hand on her cheek while admiring herself in the dress.

House of CB’s Maricel black maxi dress is adorned with feathers as well as crystals on the plunging neckline.

It costs $299 and according to the brand is best styled with black suede pumps.

“Then dress five,” Nubuke said. “OK House of CB don’t play with me.”

RUFFLE HEM LBD

The trendsetter beamed with delight as she tried on another black dress and styled her braids into a ponytail to complete the look. 

“And then dress six aww,” Nubuke said. “Look at this princess, aww cute.”

House of CB’s Lilou black ruffle hem dress has a straight cut across the bust and an eye-catching hem. 

The $255 dress is stocked in sizes ranging from XS to L+.

VELVET CORSET DRESS

Nubuke finished her try-on video with House of CB’s Arabella forest velvet corset dress for $119.

“I mean by dress seven I was like you guys gotta take my money,” she said.

“You guys gotta take my money immediately. I mean look at me.

“But then I looked at the price and I was like wow okay maybe I’ll be back later.”

She gushed about the House of CB dresses in the caption of the post as she tagged the brand with the hopes of getting a partnership. 

She wrote: “And just like that, I think my new calling in life is to be a fashion girlie.

“This House of CB haul slapped! @HouseofCB call me, I’m ready for this collab baby!”

Nubuke revealed that she preferred the dresses in a large after trying them also in a size medium.

Thousands of people liked her video and took to the comment section admitting they were eager to try the looks for themselves.

“After one it kept getting better [heart eyes],” one person wrote.
