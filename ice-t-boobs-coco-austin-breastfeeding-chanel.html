Ice-T says he also 'likes to suck mom's boobs' as he defends wife Coco Austin for still breastfeedin

RAPPER Ice-T has told fans he also “likes to suck mom’s boobs” as he defends his wife Coco Austin for still breastfeeding daughter Chanel, five.

The Law & Order: SVU star clapped back to a follower who called the continued suckling “sexual and gross.”

Ice-T, 63, is not one to mince his words and he couldn’t but clap back at a fan who voiced their opinion about his wife Coco still breastfeeding their daughter Chanel, five.

The rapper took to Twitter on Wednesday to share an "ICE Cold Fact" with his followers, it read: “Jealousy will have people hating on someone that they should be learning from.” 

However, it was one Twitter user’s comments about his family that caused sparks to fly as they replied to his statement: “Not sure that I'm jealous of a 5 yr old breastfeeding.”

Another added that they thought that Chanel still enjoying the boob was “sexual and gross,” to which the 63-year-old replied” "Why the F are you worried about MY Child??? That’s what’s weird.. Now go back in the basement." 

In another tweet he told fans: “News Flash! We feed Chanel FOOD... She just likes to suck moms boob every now and then... Me Too!!!”

As the furore calmed down he later tweeted: “​​Lol. When it’s all said and done I guess I’ll be known as a Titty Lover... I can live with that,” before adding, “Right! Shout out to EVERYONE that love to suck a titty or two!”

Earlier this month Coco Austin revealed that she still breastfeeds her five-year-old daughter Chanel despite the fact she also eats "steak and hamburgers."

Coco, 42, revealed that her child Chanel still breastfeeds, telling Us Weekly that “Chanel still likes my boobs."

“It’s a big bonding moment for a mother and your child," she claimed.

Coco explained that although her daughter does “eat steak and hamburgers,” she “likes a little snack every now and then.”

“Why take that away from her? … If she doesn’t want it, all right, that’s where you stop it. But I’m not just going to say no," she added.

The Ice Loves Coco star previously clapped back at critics who slammed her breastfeeding habit as she said the activity is "comforting" to both her and her daughter.

In March of 2020, the model wrote on Instagram that she has continued to breastfeed for "comfort," after she received some backlash from fans who disagreed with the choice.

“At a time when the world feels like it's coming to an end.. suck up as much love as you can!

“I know the moms out there will appreciate this pic!" she gushed,

"I've been getting alot of props in the breastfeeding community and get tons of emails from woman/moms appreciating me bringing light to the subject [sic].

“I write a baby blog about my journey with Chanel and soon I will write about what it's like to continue boob time with a 4-year-old.

“I get tons and tons of requests that want me to speak on it!”
