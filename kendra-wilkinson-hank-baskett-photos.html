Kendra Wilkinson &amp; Hank Basketts Romance Relived In Photos Hollywood Life

Kendra Wilkinson & Hank Baskett hold each other while posing on the red of a WE Tv event.

Kendra first revealed that she was in a relationship with the former NFL player during an interview with Chelsea Handler on her talk show in 2008. They got engaged later that year where Hank proposed to Kendra at the Space Needle in Seattle, Washington. The couple later exchanged vows at The Playboy Mansion on June 27, 2009.

She announced a couple of weeks prior to their wedding that they were expecting their first child together. Kendra later gave birth to a son named Hank Baskett Jr. in December that year. They later welcomed a daughter named Alijah in May 2014.

The family of four’s adventures with one another were often seen on her show ‘Kendra On Top’ which aired for five years beginning in 2012. She filed for divorce from him on April 3, 2018, after almost a 10-year marriage.
