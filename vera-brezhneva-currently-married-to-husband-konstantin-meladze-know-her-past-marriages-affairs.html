Vera Brezhneva Currently Married To Husband Konstantin Meladze; Know Her Past Marriages &amp; Affairs

Vera Viktorovna Galushka, best known by her stage name Vera Brezhneva is a singer based in Russia. The Russian singer is also an actress and TV presenter. The former member of girl group also was the presenter on the Russian version of game-show . She worked on the show for two seasons, starting from 7th January of 2008 until 31st August of 2008.

Today we are going to talk about the Russian actress' relationship with Konstantin Meladze and her past relationships.

Vera Brezhneva past relationships and affairs

In the year 2000, Vera was married to Ukrainian vocalist, Vitaliy Voychenko. Together they gave birth to their first child Sonya Voychenko on 30th March of 2001. Vera was just 19-years-old when she gave birth to her daughter. However, after 3 years of marriage, the couple decided to call it quit and got divorced. 

Vera Brezhneva and ex-husband Michael Kiperman

Later, in November of 2006, she got married to another Ukrainian vocalist Michael Kiperman. They gave birth to their daughter Alex Kiperman on 17th December of 2009. The two were married for 5 years until 2011.

Moreover, after her separation with Michael Kiperman, Vera was rumored being in a relationship with Russian actor, Sergei Svetlakov. The duo was rumored to have dated for a year. However, none of them ever confirmed the rumors. 

Vera Brezhneva married to husband Konstantin Meladze

The mother of two is currently married to Ukrainian composer and producer, Konstantin Meladze. The two got married in 2015. They first met when Vera was the part of the all-girl group, Nu Virgos.

Vera Brezhneva and husband Konstantin Meladze

Moreover, Vera is not Konstantin's first wife. He was previously married to Jana Meladze. With her, he welcomed 3 children, Alice Meladze born in 2000, Leah Meladze born in 2004, and Valery Meladze born in 2005. 

However, despite the past, the Ukrainian composer sure does love Vera as, during an interview, the loving husband shared that marrying Vera has changed his life completely. He shared that he admires her very much and that his wife is a very giving person.

Let's wish the couple all the very best for their future.
