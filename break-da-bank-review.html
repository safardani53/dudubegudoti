Break da Bank game overview

Break da Bank is a classic slot title that has been online for nearly a decade. The game will remind you of retro titles not only because of the 3x5 grid but because of the BAR and $ symbols. 

While there is a Wild symbol and multipliers, there are no free spins or any other type of bonus features. The reels are set upon an attractive purple background with a stack of coins below. Here is some key information about Break da Bank:

  • Developer: Microgaming (Games Global)
  • Slot type: Classic 
  • RTP: 95.75% 
  • Theme: Money and riches 
  • Reels: Three
  • Paylines: Five
  • Volatility: High 

What are the wager limits for Break da Bank slot game?

You can wager from £5 to £250 per spin, so you can see the game may be more suited to high rollers with a high minimum bet. For a win you have to hit three matching symbols on a payline, and if there are Wilds as part of the winning line they will multiply the win.

You can adjust your bet via the ‘chip’ icon, and use the ‘Coin Size’ slider. This has a minimum of 1.00 and a max of 50.00. Each value is multiplied by five, which is the number of fixed paylines.

Being an older game there are not as many features in it and the setting options are limited. The only sounds are when you spin the reels, which mimic real slot machines. There’s a ‘Quickspin’ option to speed up the reels, too.

What is the theme of Break da Bank slot game? 

The theme of Break da Bank is money and riches. One of the game's main symbols is the $ and there are piles of gold coins at the bottom of the reels. The Wild symbol is the Break da Bank logo, where behind that you will see an open vault with untold riches. 

What symbols are used in the Break da Bank slot game?

Being a classic slot with a theme of wealth, the symbols suit the game. You’ll encounter three types of BAR, the $, and the Break da Bank logo. This game pays out for three of a kind, with the following multipliers per symbol:

  • Double BARS: 8x 
  • Triple BARS: 16x 
  • $: 32x 
  • Break da Bank: 200x - 480x (for three to nine)
