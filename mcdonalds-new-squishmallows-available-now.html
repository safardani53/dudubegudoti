McDonalds fans go wild as new Squishmallows pop up now - despite claims they wouldnt be releas

A WOMAN has caused Sqiushmallow fans to go into a frenzy after revealing the mid-November toy drop has already been released.

Michelle Scammell took to social media showing off the new teddies she managed to bag before their release date.

The release of the new Happy Meal toy has left many parents scrambling to get their hands on them.

The first drop of toys were of course Halloween editions of the plushies but now they have also released classic versions as well.

Although they were thought to be releasing the six classic teddies in mid-November - it seems some lucky McDonald's fans have got their hands on them early.

Taking to the Facebook group, Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, Michelle said: "Two new Squishmallows in McDonald's."

Sharing a picture of the Happy Meal boxes it showed that she managed to grab the Tristan and Rorty plush toys.

The Squishmallow plushies can cost anywhere from £9 in stores - but the McDonald's deal means you can bag one for just £3.59 - including a meal.

And Michelle wasn't the only lucky customer who got her hands on the new collection early.

Rebecca Paragreen took to the same Facebook group chuffed she managed to get the toys for her kids.
