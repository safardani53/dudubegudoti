Sitcom legend and former EastEnders star looks unrecognisable in rare TV appearance 40 years after h

JUST Good Friends star Paul Nicholas looked very different as he appeared on Loose Women yesterday.

The 78-year-old became a household name played Vincent in the hit sitcom from 1983 to 1986 -  but his curly blonde hair is long gone.

He is also well known for being in EastEnders as Kathy Beale's third husband Gavin Sullivan.

Those watching Loose Women couldn’t get over how different he looked, with one person commenting: “Omg how can this be him!”

Another added: “Wow he looks older than I would of expected?”

But one viewer hit back: “Well I'm absolutely disgusted with these comments about how he's got old. He's still very striking for an old man and he has such a gorgeous personality. He is charming and kind, presents himself very well. People can be do rude!”

During his interview on Loose Women,  Paul opened up about how he met his wife Linzi, who he has been married to since 1984.

He said: “She was on tour with me in Hair. And you know, the girls that you really like, you can never speak to you’re always too shy. I was anyway.

“I managed to manoeuvre myself in the flat above where she was staying and she used to come up and watch TV after the show and I’d make wisecracks and things.

“Anyway, one night she went down and I hadn’t made a move on her, but I was staying with the chap who played Paul Barber, who was in Only Fools and Horses.
