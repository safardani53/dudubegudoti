I won $255 on scratch-off tickets see the exact ones and how many I bought

A LUCKY winner has scored $255 after playing an entire book of scratch-off tickets.

YouTuber Get Rich or Die Scratching (GRODS) has been playing scratch-offs for years, winning some major cash along the way. 

He's known for playing all types of books like Gold Rush, Gigantic Jumbo Bucks, Win It All, and many more.

Recently, he posted a YouTube sharing one of his favorite games where he won $255.

Monopoly Doubler

The game is called the Monopoly Doubler Florida Lottery Ticket and like usual, he bought the entire book.

The book is a $600 gamble and each ticket is worth $20.

The lottery player and his buddy split the pack, equally putting in $300.

The pack is stocked with 30 tickets and has an ultimate prize of $5million.

GRODS said the odds are printed on the back of each ticket but are always in the house’s favor - 1:3.02.

These are the odds for all tickets ever printed.

In a pack of 30 tickets, there should only be 10 winners.

This means if you get more than 10 wins, you beat the odds.

How to play

The Monopoly Doubler Florida Lottery Ticket is one of the best books GRODS has played in months.

For this particular scratch-off, if the doubler box matches a number to one of the winning numbers printed on the ticket, you get double the prize.

Each symbol correlates to a specific prize amount and can be found at the bottom of each ticket.

GRODS said in the video he was fully prepared to lose at least half of their money but did expect to win at least $300 back.

After playing the entire book, he ended up winning 13 tickets beating the odds.

Overall he won $855 for a total profit of $255.

More scratcher strategies

Scratch-off games account for nearly 75% of sales generating over $1.3billion for the Educational Enhancement Trust Fund in the fiscal year 2021-22, according to the Florida Lottery.

Scratchers are a quick and easy way to win but before you play - do your research.

Since scratchers are distributed to retailers in large rolls, there is a guaranteed number of winners within it.

Meaning if the game has been around for a while, it's best to do a quick Google search to see if someone has already won one of the larger prizes.

Additionally, it might be best to follow the GRODS method of buying in bulk.

This will increase your odds since the manufacturers place a certain number of winners in each roll.

Lastly, pick a game and then stick to that one, especially if someone hasn't claimed the big prize yet.

Each time you or someone else loses, you instantly increase your odds of winning.
