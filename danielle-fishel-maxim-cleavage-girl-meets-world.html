Danielle Fishel Will Reprise Her Role As Topanga In ‘Girl Meets World’

The sexy star will revisit her role as Topanga in the upcoming Boy Meets World spin-off, Girl Meets World. Her sexy toned legs and seductive look will surely entice anyone to watch the new series. 

Danielle Reveals Topanga’s Origins

Danielle tells Maxim magazine how her character got her eccentric name. She explains to the mag, “Michael Jacobs, who created Boy Meets World, says he was driving down the highway when production called and said, ‘We need a name for this character!’ He happened to be driving past Topanga Canyon, so he said, ‘Topanga. He says that if they had called him two miles later, I would’ve been named Canoga, which is the next exit.”

Danielle Dated Ben Savage? The Truth Revealed

Danielle also clears up rumors about her dating former co-star Ben Savage, who played Cory Mathews on the BMW series. She said, “That rumor was entirely my fault because I once said, on The Tyra Banks Show, that Ben and I went on a date, and that turned into ‘Danielle and Ben dated,'” she explains. “We went on one date, when I was about 15, and by the time the dinner was over we realized mutually that we were more like family than lovers.”
