Shoppers rush to buy dream pastel pink suit from Primark that's perfect for spring - and it's cheap

WITH Easter Sunday just around the corner, the hunt is on for the perfect spring outfit.

And fashion fans have already got their eye on a colourful pink suit from Primark.

Bosses at the bargain store's branch in East Kilbride, Lanarkshire, shared a TikTok clip of the pastel co-ord yesterday.

They said: "How stunning is our gorgeous new pink suit? Perfect for spring."

The outfit doesn't work out too expensive either with the blazer priced at £22 and accompanying shorts or trousers at £12 and £14.

And shoppers are desperate to get their hands on the pastel outfit.

The store's clip has racked up more than 325,000 views in less than a day.

One fashionista said: "Get me to Primark ASAP."

A second gushed: "I’m in love."

A third wrote: "If I can find this for Sunday, I’m getting it."

A fourth added: "We need to go to Primark. That’s what I’m on about with black lace bra underneath."

Meanwhile, another insisted: "Another Primark trip soon. I need the shorts."

Elsewhere, a size 18 fashion fan has revealed she felt like a "stuffed sausage" after picking up a huge Primark haul.

Rachel Spicer, from Falkirk, regularly shares her shopping hauls and outfit choices with her 2.3 million followers on TikTok (@rachelspicer_)

In a recent video, the 25-year-old tried on some of Primark's newest releases for spring - filling five bags with her purchases.

But not everything was a hit for the size 18 influencer.

After trying on the the viral seamless bike shorts and crop top set in a bright blue, Rachel exclaimed: "I feel naked. I feel exposed. I feel like a stuffed sausage."
