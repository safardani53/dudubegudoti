Meet Christina Ricci's Boyfriend James Heerdegen!

Christina Ricci has a new man in her life!

The 31-year-old actress is dating dolly grip James Heerdegen, Us Weekly can exclusively report. The two met on the set of the actress’ ABC series Pan Am.

To celebrate their hot new romance, the couple recently spent time at the W Retreat & Spa-Vieques Island in Puerto Rico, staying in a luxe ocean front WOW suite. (The romantic destination is the same place where Ben Flajnik and Courtney Robertson went skinny dipping on The Bachelor!)

“They lounged on the deck of their room a lot,” an insider says, adding that they “walked up and down” W Beach frequently. “Christina was really active and did a lot of swimming.”
