Who is Cameron Mathison's wife Vanessa?

Vanessa Mathison was born on April 19, 1967, in Puerto Rico and now resides in Los Angeles, California.

She is a model signed with LA Models Direct and has been married to actor Cameron Mathison since July 27, 2002.

According to her Instagram bio, Vanessa is of Mexican and Filipino descent and considers herself to be a "beginner bassist."

Vanessa made repeated appearances on Hallmark Channel's talk show Home & Family, which Cameron co-hosted alongside Debbie Matenopoulos.
