I work on The Repair Shop and there's a secret outfit code linked to the items

THE Repair Shop's resident hatter Jayesh Vaghela has opened up on his style secrets.

He is known for his smart attire on the popular BBC One programme which he stars in alongside the likes of Jay Blades.

Jayesh recently took to Instagram in a video for The Repair Shop's official account.

He said: "When you are watching the show, you may have noticed that I always tend to wear a bow tie.

"Now the thing is when I start on a new item, the colour of the bow tie changes. So look out for that."

Away from the popular BBC TV series, the personality works as an as assistant manager in London's Lock & Co.

Founded in 1676, it's known as the world's oldest hat shop, proving that he has much expertise in the highest levels in his field.

After making his debut five years ago in 2018, he returned to The Repair Shop for its twelfth instalment.

In an episode of the programme last year, he explained that he learnt the basics in sewing from his older sibling.

He explained: "As I was growing up, my eldest sister, she was very good at hand embroidery. As a child, I would be sat right next to her playing with my toys."
