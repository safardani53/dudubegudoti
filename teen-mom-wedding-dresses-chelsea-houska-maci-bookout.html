TEEN Mom fans have been treated to several weddings throughout the show's tenure, which means tons of different wedding gowns.

Chelsea Houska, Leah Messer, Catelynn Lowell, and more have tied the knot in front of MTV's cameras.

Teen Mom has featured many major milestones for its stars through the years, but none are quite as special as seeing the stars get married.

In addition to getting to share in a special moment in the cast's lives, fans have loved seeing the wedding dresses they choose.

Some, like Catelynn, Chelsea, and Leah, have gone the traditional route, walking down the aisle in gorgeous white gowns.

Others, like Maci, have bucked tradition and worn unique colors and styles on their big day.

Among the first to tie the knot on Teen Mom was Leah, 29.

Fans watched in season one as she and now ex-husband Corey Simms exchanged vows in front of family and friends.

Three seasons later, she married Jeremy Calvert, whom she also later divorced.

Other noteworthy Teen Mom weddings include Kailyn Lowry and Javi Marroquin's nuptials, and Mackenzie and Josh McKee's I dos.

Although not every Teen Mom marriage has lasted, the memories live on in old episodes and fans' memories.

Here's a look at some of the wedding gowns Teen Mom stars have worn through the years.

CHELSEA HOUSKA

Chelsea, 30, didn't film her wedding for MTV, but did capture the days leading up to the day.

Fans got a glimpse at her gorgeous wedding gown after she and husband Cole DeBoer exchanged vows.

The couple opened up to Life & Style about their wedding day.

Chelsea, who recently landed a show with her man on HGTV, also revealed on social media where she got her gown.

The South Dakota native traveled to Minneapolis, Minnesota months before her walk down the aisle.

She purchased the gown from L'atelier Couture bridal shop.

The mom of four revealed that she would be wearing ivory or nude on her wedding day.

She told Life & Style of her day: "We're having a rustic wedding in South Dakota."

Her dress, which she showed off after the fact, reflected the theme.

She stunned next to her husband in an A-line dress with ties at the shoulders.

Her long hair – then dyed her signature red – was worn in a half-up, half-down style.

The dress featured a flowing train and lace details.

MACI BOOKOUT

Maci is known for being untraditional and her wedding dress was no different.

She and Taylor McKinney married in 2016 in Greenville, Florida.

The Tennessee native shared photos from her big day with Us Weekly.

Rather than a white gown, she walked down the aisle in rose gold gown from Riki Dalal Haute Couture.

The dress was strapless with a sweetheart neckline and an A-line cut.

The bottom was made of a loose flowing material that hung down over her feet.

Daughter Jade served as flower girl at the wedding, while son Maverick, only four months at the time, say in a wagon that was pulled down the aisle.

At the time, she and Taylor had been together for four years.

A month before the wedding, Maci and Taylor shared their engagement photos.

The theme of the shoot was keeping each other safe and grounded.

They shot several different photos, including one where Taylor appears to be floating away with a bunch of balloons while Maci grabs at the string.

In another, Taylor is seen lifting his wife up in the air while she points her toes.

A third photo showed them arm wrestling.

CATELYNN LOWELL

Catelynn and Tyler Baltierra tied the knot after many years of dating and several children together.

The couple had been together for 10 years when they said "I do" in 2015.

According to Us Weekly, the parents of four married at Castle Farms in Charlevoix, Michigan.

Catelynn said her vows in a lace ballgown style dress.

She told OK! Magazine of the gown: "It's elegant, mature and makes me feel beautiful."

The dress was white with a sweetheart neckline covered in a sheer fabric with lace detailing on the sleeves.

It also boasted a blinged out belt at her waist and a flowing train.

Catelynn wore her hair in curls and capped the look off with a bright red lipstick.

KAILYN LOWRY

Teen Mom fans have seen many of Kailyn's relationships on the MTV series through the years, but she's only walked down the aisle once.

During an early season of the show, viewers watched as she and Javi tied the knot.

In 2013, Kailyn and her ex married in the presence of family and friends.

They held their wedding at Adventure Aquarium in Camden, New Jersey.

In Touch Weekly got the first look at her wedding dress, which was made of lace.

The wedding came after the couple secretly married at a Pennsylvania court house.

Kailyn's tattoos – as well as her baby bump – were on full display in the gown.

She married her ex in a sleeveless dress that hugged her belly and hung just to her feet.

Oldest son Isaac was in the wedding, which took place while Kailyn was pregnant with Lincoln.

The podcast host told In Touch of her nuptials: "The whole ceremony was magical.

"Being with our friends and family made it real."

LEAH MESSER

Leah and Corey married in season one of Teen Mom 2.

The mom of three walked down the aisle in a $1,549 Maggie Sottero wedding dress, according to The Daily Mail.

Daughters Ali and Aleeah served as flower girls at the event, which took place at a park lodge not far from their home in West Virginia.

Their marriage lasted just six months.

A few seasons later, Leah married Jeremy.

The two wed in an intimate ceremony at a wedding chapel in Cattlesburg, Kentucky.

Fans saw the duo marry in a beach ceremony in South Carolina, however.

Roughly 30 guests attended the destination wedding, getting drenched in the rain on the big day.

Leah wore a white gown for the occasion, OK! Magazine reported.

MACKENZIE MCKEE

Mackenzie and Josh – high school sweethearts – married in 2013 in a country-themed wedding.

According to Us Weekly, the fired MTV star wore a white gown with a rhinestone-studded corset and ruffled high-low hem.
