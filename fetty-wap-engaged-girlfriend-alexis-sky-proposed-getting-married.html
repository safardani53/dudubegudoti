Fetty Wap Engaged To Alexis Sky? Rapper May Have Proposed To Longtime Girlfriend Hollywood Life

Although no details of the proposal have been revealed, multiple sources close to Fetty told MediaTakeOut that the rapper is indeed engaged. According to their report, Alexis showed off her sparkler on Snapchat on March 22. Unfortunately, though, she hasn’t shared any ring pics on Instagram. Sneaky!

If the reports are true and Fetty is engaged, we couldn’t be happier for him and Alexis! It’s especially great to hear that Fetty has found love after all the relationship drama he has dealt with in the last few months. First, Fetty’s former flame, Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood star, Masika Kalysha, called out Alexis for dating her ex after the model posted a picture with Fetty in her bed. Then just one month later, Masika revealed that she was six months pregnant, hinting that it was Fetty’s baby!

Upon Masika’s pregnancy announcement, Fetty quickly denied her claim and demanded a paternity test. Although nothing has come of that now, clearly Fetty has moved on — and is ready to start a (hopefully) drama-free life with Alexis!

Whether Masika’s baby actually is Fetty’s, either way Alexis will be a step-mommy. Fetty has little girl from his relationship with ex-girlfriend Lezhae Zeona. If Fetty did propose and Alexis accepted, she’s obviously ready to take on the stepmom role! Other than Alexis’ supposed ring flaunting on Snapchat, neither Fetty nor Alexis have confirmed their engagement. We’re certainly dying to know if it’s true!
