Shoppers joke Missguideds 10 extreme thong swimsuit is the definition of business in the fro

LOVE Island stars and Instagram models have helped make thong bikinis all the rage – but a new swimsuit by Missguided has been slammed as going too far.

The fashion company shocked customers by selling a boot-exposing swimsuit that looks like the back is made entirely of string.

Leaving nothing to the imagination is their Red Square Neck G String Thong Swimsuit, which sells for £10 despite the lack of material.

From the front, the red-coloured swimming costume looks fairly standard and features a high-leg cut.

However, if you turn around, the swimsuit features an extreme G-string fit and has two tiny criss-cross straps, making it one of the raciest on the market.

Baffled shoppers have taken to Twitter to share their shock at the flesh-exposing suit.

  • Red Square Neck G String Thong Swimsuit, £10 from Missguided - buy now

One uploaded a photo of the cossie, with the caption: “If this ‘swimsuit’ isn’t the definition of ‘business in the front, party in the back’ then I don’t know what is.”

Another said: “That ain't a swim suit, that's lingerie.”

And one queried: “I’m actuality quite intrigued to know if people would actually wear this.”

It’s currently available in all sizes except for 14, and comes in three colours; red, dark green and black.
