Margaret Qualley Hollywood Life

Bio:
Margaret Qualley (born Sarah Margaret Qualley on October 23, 1994) is an American actress who was raised in Asheville, North Carolina. As a young debutante, Margaret trained as a ballerina with some of the best professional dancers in the world. After an apprenticeship at the American Ballet Theater and studying at New York’s French Academy, she was offered another apprenticeship with the North Carolina Dance Theater company. However, she turned it down to attend London’s Royal Academy of Dramatic Art so she could pursue a life of acting. Margaret’s first role ever was in the indie drama Palo Alto.

Best Known For:
Her role as Jillian Garvey on The Leftovers.

Personal Life:
Aside from being the daughter of famed actress Andie MacDowell, very little is known about Margaret’s personal life.
