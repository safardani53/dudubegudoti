Shoppers think they've found the ultimate Ugg Tazz dupes in Deichmann - and you'll save an eye-water

UGG’S Tazz clogs may have sold out everywhere - but you can nab dupes of them at a high-street shoe store. 

And the best bit? You’ll save yourself a whopping £85. 

The Ugg Tazzs have been incredibly hard to get your mitts on for weeks now.

Resellers are even flogging them on auction sites like eBay and Depop for DOUBLE the price due to the sky-high demand.

But you can nab a dupe of fur-lined shoes for just £19.99 at Deichmann. 

The mammoth shoe retailer even has the same three colours: black, chestnut and chocolate.

According to Ugg bosses, the Tazz shoe is “the clog you've been looking for”. 

But with them priced at £105, shoppers have been hunting for dupes. 

Deichmann’s versions are available in sizes 3 to 8 but can’t be found online, meaning you’ll have to hunt to your local store to find a pair. 

Fashion fans have been sharing snaps from inside the high-street stores on social media, revealing towers of show boxes containing the Ugg dupes.

Both Deichmann and Ugg's designs feature a platform sole, which gives the wearer extra height while still cushioning the foot.

Plus, they both feature super soft fur lining, the Aztec style detail around the ankle and the centre seam running down the bridge of the foot.

“The platform outsole gives you that of the now lifted vibe, with a secret-sturdy step-in feel,” Ugg bosses said. 

“Our ultra-lush lining and insole will wrap your feet in moisture-wicking dreamy softness.”

“Somehow I could imagine these as mega cuddly slippers,” one shopper said on Instagram. 

“I need to grab a pair of these ASAP,” another penned. 

“They’re beautiful.”
