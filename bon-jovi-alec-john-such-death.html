Who was Alec John Such?

FORMED in 1983, Bon Jovi is a famous American rock 'n' roll band led by the titular singer Jon Bon Jovi.

On Sunday, June 5, 2022, Bon Jovi took to Twitter to announce the death of Alec John Such.

Who was Alec John Such?

Born on November 14, 1952, Alec John Such was a native of Yonkers, New York.

He played as the bassist in Bon Jovi from the band's inception in 1983 until over a decade later, in 1994, when he left the group.

Aside from bass, Such lent backing vocals.

After leaving the group, he continued to work in music.

Such managed several bands across the United States, including local groups in New Jersey and the Chicago-based 7th Heaven.

What happened to Alec John Such?

The band wrote: "We are heartbroken to hear the news of the passing of our dear friend Alec John Such.

"He was an original. As a founding member of Bon Jovi, Alec was integral to the formation of the band."
