KIM Kardashian's daughter North West seemed to have subtly sent her dad Kanye West a message through her outfit, fans thought.

The young 10-year-old star donned a bedazzled jacket at her mom Kim Kardashian's Christmas Eve bash, but fans noticed a strange detail.

North posed for photos alongside her mom Kim and her siblings, Saint, Chicago, and Psalm.

North smiled widely and matched her outfit with her mom as she donned a long sequined silver denim jacket.

She paired the look with white pants and boots and accessorized with a huge silver chain at her neck.

Fans noticed something in the photo and took to a popular social media thread to share their thoughts.

A user uploaded the photo with Kim and North next to an old image of Kanye and Kim at the 2016 Met Gala.

North wore Kanye's jacket from the event.

The user titled the Reddit thread: "North West wore Kanye's Balmain jacket from the 2016 Met Gala for Christmas."

They added: "Thought yall would love this as much as I did."

RANTING

North wearing Kanye's jacket came after the star went on a wild rant and suggested that his daughter ripped up furniture to see him.

Kanye teamed up with rapper Ty Dolla $ign to host a rave for their upcoming album - which was later shut down by police.

After storming out of the event, he was filmed giving a heated 10-minute speech to a crowded room full of people.

He yelled: "Jesus Christ, Hitler, Ye! Sponsor that!"

"My daughter ripped up the mother f**king couches in the house to be able to be with me right now," Kanye continued.

North was with Kanye earlier in the week, joining him onstage to perform a verse from his new album.

SECOND HAND?

This was not the first time North donned a garment of her father's, with the young television star wearing an ultra-rare jacket last year.

The star remains close with her father and often wears his clothes around her mom, who has yet to respond publicly to Kanye's rant.
