‘Duck Dynasty’ Stars Didn’t Always Have Beards

In 2001, you would’ve hardly been able recognize the Robertson men, who are now quite famous for their wildly long facial hair on their show Duck Dynasty. But they weren’t always that way, as we can see in a new photo revealed by Life&Style on Oct. 18.

This is pretty shocking — like seeing Santa Claus without a beard!

The four brothers — Willie, Alan, Jase and Jep — may have adopted a more rugged look over the years, but a family friend says they’ve remained the same on the inside.

‘Duck Dynasty’ Doubles Down On Family Drama

Besides their beards, the Robertson clan is known for drama and their unusual ways of dealing with problems. The nearly 12 million viewers who tune in to watch everything unfold on A&E have thrust these reportedly down to earth stars into the spotlight, which in turn is causing some family tension.

But, regardless of the family feuds, you can count on one thing — those beards aren’t going anywhere!
