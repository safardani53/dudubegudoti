Who is Marc Daly from Real Housewives of Atlanta?

REAL Housewives of Atlanta star Kenya Moore and her husband Marc Daly called it quits only one year after they were married.

They first announced their separation in 2019 and filed for divorce in May 2021.

Who is Marc Daly?

Marc Daly rose to fame after he began dating Kenya Moore, a former Miss USA and The Real Housewives of Atlanta star.

Before his relationship with Moore became public, Daly worked at Credit Suisse First Bolton Bank in New York City.

Daly then moved to work for Citibank as an investment banker, before moving up to the vice president position in the company.

He moved on from the world of banking and decided to take a chance at the restaurant business, where he established his successful southern fusion restaurant, SoCo, in Brooklyn.

What is Marc Daly's net worth?

Daly earned his net worth from his work in banking and from his successful restaurant business.

His exact net worth is unknown, with outlets reporting the range to be as widespread as $4million to $35million.

SoCo was launched to cater to blue-collar workers in New York and is intended to be a restaurant and cocktail bar.

The restaurant's designer, Andres Aladin, describes the restaurant on the website as: "..the Brooklyn Bridge turned into a restaurant. We wanted to play off of the design cues of the space and the history of the neighborhood being very industrial."

When did Marc Daly and Kenya Moore separate?

Daly released a statement in January 2021, that his marriage to the Bravo star has ended for the second time following a failed attempt to rekindle their romance. 

“After recently attending mediation, Kenya and I have agreed to end our marriage,” he told The Root. 

He continued: "I will always have great care for her and look forward to amicably continuing to co-parent our daughter Brooklyn with an abundance of love and wisdom.

The pair first announced they split in September 2019 but later reconciled. 

How can I watch Real Housewives of Atlanta?
