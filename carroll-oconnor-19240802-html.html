Carroll O'Connor (TV Actor) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

John Carroll O'Connor is an American actor, producer, and director whose television career spans four decades. A lifetime member of Actors Studio, in 1971, O'Connor was widely known as Archie Bunker, the main character of the CBS sitcom All in the Family and its spinoff, Archie Bunker's Place. O'Connor went on to star in the NBC / CBS crime drama In the Heat of the Night, where he played Sparta, Mississippi, Sheriff William "Bill" Gillespie. At the end of his career in the late 1990s, he played Gus Stemple, the father of Jamie Buchman in Mad About You. In 1996, O'Connor was ranked 38th on TV Guide's list of the 50 Greatest Television Stars of All Time. He won 5 Emmy Awards and two Golden Globe Awards.
