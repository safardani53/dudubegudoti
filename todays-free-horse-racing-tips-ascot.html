Today's FREE horse racing tips: Ascot, Pontefract, Brighton, Wolverhampton and Bath - Steve Mullen's

ASCOT

2.00

THE IRISH ROVER should take a big step forward from his debut at Dundalk three weeks ago.

He was very green there and ran on well enough in the closing stages. That was a good start and he looks hard to beat.

2.35

DATHANNA has already won three of her past four races and was impressive on her comeback at Kempton last time, running right away from her rivals.

That was over this mile and she gives the strong impression that there’s more to come.

3.10

TORCEDOR didn’t go a yard in Meydan last time which was a surprise because his form was strong last year.

He finished second to Order Of St George in the Irish St Leger and at Ascot so there’s no problem with this 2m trip. He likes the track too and soft ground won’t be an issue.

3.45 - Mullen's star bet (add to your betslip)

INVINCIBLE ARMY was highly consistent last season and held his own in better races than this.

He signed off last term with an unlucky second in the Group 2 Mill Reef at Newbury where he was blocked at a crucial point in the race.

He relishes this trip and has gone well with some cut underfoot.

4.20

CENTURY DREAM ran no sort of race in a Longchamp Group 2 in bottomless conditions on his comeback last time out.

He proved himself to be a high-class handicapper last season, finishing with a game win over 1m2f at Newbury. He looks capable of stepping into this level and will be fitter today.

4.55

ABAREEQ is right down the weights compared to his all-weather mark and that’s before his rider takes another 7lb off.

He is fit from a spin around Chelmsford where he was beaten in a photo and this trip is right up his street.
