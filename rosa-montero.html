What is Rosa Montero known for?

Born in Madrid in 1951, Rosa Montero has been a journalist for Madrid's daily newspaper, El Pais, since 1976. She has published 11 novels, many of which have been bestsellers in Spain. Beautiful and Dark is the third of Montero's novels to be translated into English.

What are some fun facts about Rosa Montero?

The daughter of a bullfighter and a housewife, Montero was born in Cuatro Caminos, a district of Madrid. The contraction of tuberculosis forced her to remain at home between the ages of five and nine, and she began reading and writing extensively during that time.
