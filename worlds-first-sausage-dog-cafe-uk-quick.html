The worlds first sausage dog caf is coming to the UK but you'll need to be quick to book a s

DOG lovers rejoice, the world's very first dachshund café is coming to the UK.

It's a one-off event, so sausage-dog lovers will need to have their paws at the ready if they want to get involved.

Owners are invited to bring their furry friends, while the rest of us can simply get involved in the petting and cuddling action.

The event is being organised by the team behind the Pug Café, who have run pop-up events in Manchester, London, Nottingham, Surrey, Liverpool, Birmingham, Brighton and beyond.

Now Esquires Guildford is being transformed into a sausage dog paradise for one day only on Sunday March 25.

There will be a whole dachshund menu on offer - including pupcakes, bark-scotti, woofins and a free pupuccino for every sausage dog.

Meanwhile, us humans can tuck into a range of cakes, pastries, hot food, smoothies and coffees.

The event costs £13 for Dachshund Lovers and a fiver for Dachshund Owners.

Booking opens on Monday at 9am - when two email addresses will be released. Then it's first-come, first-served for booking a competitive spot.

You need to email these details

  • Your full name
  • How many dachshunds you're bringing, plus their names and a photo
  • Contact number
  • Human group size – of up to four, if you're a family with kids
  • Your 1st and 2nd preferred time slots - or say you're flexible (but only if you really are!)

    • Slots are 70 minutes each - at 8am, 9.10am, 10.20am, 11.30am, 12.45pm, 2pm, 3.10pm, 4.20pm and 5.30pm.

    It can take up to 72 hours for the team to get back to you and confirm your spot, and it's sure to be a sell-out, so make sure you're on the ball on Monday.
