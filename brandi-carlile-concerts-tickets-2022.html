Brandi Carlile concerts 2022: How can I buy tickets?

COUNTRY singer, Brandi Carlile, is a six-time Grammy award winner.

She'll be back on the road this coming June for the second round of her Beyond These Silent Days tour.

Is Brandi Carlile on tour?

Brandi Carlile, 40, will return to the stage this summer with special guests Sarah McLachlan, Lucius, Indigo Girls, Celisse, Ani DeFranco, Allison Russell, and Brittany Howard.

Her first show will be on June 11, 2022, at the Gorge Amphitheatre in Washington and will wrap up on October 22 at Madison Square Garden in New York.

Carlile's In These Silent Days tour will visit some of the biggest stadiums in cities like Los Angeles, Boston, Nashville and more.

She is expected to sing hits from her latest album including You and Me On The Rock and Stay Gentle.
