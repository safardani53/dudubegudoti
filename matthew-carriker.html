Career

Matthew Carriker is a well-known YouTuber and social media celebrity who has made a big impression with his entertaining work. Matthew’s YouTube career started on April 16, 2007, when he launched his “OffTheRanch” channel. Carriker rose to prominence via his daily vlogs, mainly for his repair videos of repossessed houses and cars.

On January 3, 2011, he upped his content creation while pursuing his degree by launching the “DemolitionRanch” channel. This second channel focused on gun-related programs, such as product reviews and shooting demonstrations. Carriker failed to graduate and had to postpone his plans to create a veterinary clinic because he spent too much time on YouTube recording and uploading films. He prioritized his study and took a break from video production. After completing his graduate studies, the social media celebrity founded a veterinary facility before returning to YouTube.

As a consequence of this shift in concentration, Matthew launched his third YouTube channel, “Vet Ranch,” on January 28, 2014. His work as a veterinarian was featured on the channel, which also encouraged adoption and animal welfare. Carriker’s dedication to creating entertaining and educational videos paid off, as his YouTube channels amassed millions of fans. His ascent to fame was propelled by his appealing personality and abilities as a veterinarian and gun aficionado. The online celebrity has gained popularity on social media as a result of the success of his channels, which have a large following.
