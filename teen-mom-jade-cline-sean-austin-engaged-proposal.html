TEEN Mom star Jade Cline and her baby daddy Sean Austin are engaged, a trailer for the new season has revealed.

Take a look inside Sean’s elaborate proposal to his long-term on-off girlfriend, which was filmed by MTV.

On Thursday, the network released a trailer for the new edition of the franchise - Teen Mom: The Next Chapter.

In the clip, fans leaned that Sean had popped the question to Jade, with whom he shares a four-year-old daughter named Kloie.

From the preview, fans could see that Sean took a lot of steps to set the perfect scene for the proposal.

First, he arranged a huge display of letters spelling out “marry me” which he decorated with bunches of red roses and huge white candles.

He got down on one knee in front of Jade’s friends and family, including their daughter.

“Jade, will you marry me?” he asked in the teaser clip.

A video shared by Jade on TikTok revealed that she said yes.

In the caption, the soon-to-be Mrs Austin wrote: “Our happily ever after has just begun. So glad we can finally share this.”

She continued: “We worked for this and I’m so proud of where we are. Love always prevails.”

Taking to Instagram, Jade showed off her silver and crystal engagement ring, gushing, “Sean did so good!”

DATE IN THE DIARY

In a Q&A with her fans, the reality star revealed more details about their upcoming wedding.

She confirmed that the engagement took place in July, but they kept it under wraps because they “didn’t want to ruin the clip for the new season”.

The happy couple has already made solid plans for the wedding.

They have booked a venue for October 2023, and the occasion will have a “gothic victorian” theme.

But fans “will have to see” if Jade invites any of her Teen Mom costars to the wedding.

The Indiana-native also hinted that their big day will “of course” be filmed for an MTV special.

ROCKY ROAD

Although things appear to be going great between Jade and Sean, it’s been a long journey to get there.

Sean has battled with addiction issues, but made the decision to get sober and completed a stint in rehab earlier this year.
