Hardwell (DJ) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Robbert van de Corput, known as Hardwell, is a Dutch DJ, record producer and remixer from Breda, North Brabant. Hardwell was voted the world's number one DJ on DJ Mag in 2013 and again in 2014. He was ranked 12th by DJ Mag in the Top 100 DJ 2019 poll. Hardwell is best known for his sets at music festivals including Ultra Music Festival, Sunburn and Tomorrowland. Hardwell was first recognized in 2009 with the title "Show Me Love vs. Be". He founded Revealed Recordings in 2010 and a podcast and radio show Hardwell On Air in 2011. He has released 10 compilation albums through his label, as well as a documentary. His first studio album, United We Are, was released in 2015.
