Gregg Williams is an American football coach. He was most recently the defense coordinator of the New York Jets of the National Football League from 2019-2020. Williams is known for his aggressive 4–3 offensive disposition, putting great pressure on opposing midfielders and for his central role in the New Orleans Saints bonus scandal. In March 2012, Williams was indefinitely suspended from the NFL for admitting his involvement in the New Orleans Saints bonus scandal, in which bonuses were paid for the injury caused by players.
