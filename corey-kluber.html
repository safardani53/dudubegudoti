Who is Corey Kluber:

Corey Kluber is a famous Baseball Player. He was born on April 10, 1986 and his birthplace is Birmingham, AL. Corey is also well known as, MLB pitcher who won the American League Cy Young Award with the Cleveland Indians in both 2014 and 2017. He helped the Indians reach the 2016 World Series where they were defeated by the Chicago Cubs in seven games.

Corey is originated from United States. While he won the 2014 Cy Young Award for the American League, Dodgers pitcher Clayton Kershaw was claiming his third career Cy Young Award for the National League.
