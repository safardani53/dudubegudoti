Happy birthday SZA! See some of the singer’s best photos here.

SZA (real name Solána Imani Rowe) is a Grammy-nominated singer known for many of her hit songs like “Love Galore,” “The Weekend” and “Broken Clocks.” She broke into the music scene with her self-released EP See.SZA.Run, and her debut album, Ctrl, arrived in 2017. The wait was well worth it — the album was met with critical acclaim and led to many Grammy nominations the following year for honors like “Best Urban Contemporary Album,” “Best R&B Song” and “Best New Artist.” SZA is also known for her chart-topping collab with Kendrick Lamar, “All the Stars,” and her collab with Maroon 5, “What Lovers Do.”

SZA is currently at work on her second studio album. In 2020, SZA teamed up with Justin Timberlake to deliver the lead single on the Trolls World Tour soundtrack, “The Other Side.”
