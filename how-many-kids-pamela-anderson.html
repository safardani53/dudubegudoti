Pamela Anderson kids: Who are Brandon Thomas Lee and Dylan Jagger Lee?

CANADIAN-AMERICAN actress, model, and activist, Pamela Anderson has been married four times over the years.

During Pamela's first marriage with Tommy Lee, she welcomed two children, including Brandon Thomas Lee and Dylan Jagger Lee.

The former couple's children include:

Is Pamela Anderson married?

Pamela and the Mötley Crüe musician tied the knot in 1995 after knowing each other for only four days.

At the time, Pamela was Tommy's third wife as he had been married to Elaine Starchuk and Heather Locklear prior.

After getting married at a beachside reception, the couple decided to part ways in 1998, after three years of marriage.

"There was Tommy and then there was nobody else. He was the love of my life. We had a wild and crazy beginnings that was too much for both of us," Pamela told PEOPLE in 2015.

"It really was love at first sight. I only knew him four days before I married him.

She then married Kid Rock in 2006 and divorced him just four months later.

Her third husband is Rick Salomon, whom she was married to for two months in 2007 and from 2014 to 2015, per StyleCaster.

Anderson then married her fourth husband, producer Jon Peters, for less than two weeks in 2020 before marrying bodyguard Dan Hayhurst in 2021.

As of the time of writing, Pamela is not married.

Who is Pamela Anderson?

Pamela Anderson, born on July 1, 1967, is a Canadian-American actress and model known for her Playboy magazine shoots.

Anderson first entered the media when she was featured on the stadium screen at a British Columbia Lions football game.

This is how Playboy got a hold of Anderson and she was later featured on Playboy's cover in October 1989.
