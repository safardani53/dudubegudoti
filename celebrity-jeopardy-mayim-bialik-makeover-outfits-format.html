Jeopardy! fans go wild over leaked first photos of Mayim Bialik in full couture for Celebrity Jeopar

MAYIM Bialik has looked unrecognizable in full glam and couture for the first Celebrity Jeopardy! promo pics, the spinoff has revealed Friday.

Fans went wild over her makeover for its new official poster and a second leaked promo shot.

Season 39 of Jeopardy! is hitting the small screen in just days, premiering on Monday, September 12th.

Former champ Ken Jennings, 48, and actress Mayim, 46, were announced as its dual hosts and the permanent successors for the late, great Alex Trebek in the meantime.

However, it's just Mayim who is handling its glitzy celebrity spinoff.

Starting September 25th, Celebrity Jeopardy! will air on Sundays and not interfere with regular episodes - and Mayim certainly looked up for the task in the new snaps.

In the sensationally shiny official poster revealed on Friday, Mayim stood behind giant Hollywood-sign letters reading "Celebrity Jeopardy!" - as well as some palm trees.

She stunned with a red lip, editorial white blazer, flare-out white pants to match, a billowing blue top, and chic high heels.

"The classic quiz show is going Hollywood," the poster promised.

ABC also quietly leaked a promotional image of just Mayim standing against a grey backdrop with painted nails, a bold pink blazer, matching top, short skirt and wild flourescant heels.

'SUPER SHARP'

"You look great!" one Instagram user gushed on the first snap.

"Look at that super sharp-looking host," wrote another.

A third wrote as her pink dress look strutted across Twitter that hasn't been shared by the show yet: "OK YES, this is a great color on the Jeopardy! stage"

"She usually wears neutral color shoes for regular Jeopardy, gotta spice things up for Celebrity Jeopardy!"

'DISAPPOINTING CAST'

The primetime spinoff will feature, so far, eight celebrities at the podiums instead of your average trivia-toting citizens; per TV Line, the episodes will run one hour, not 30 minutes.

In the first flashy trailer earlier released, Mayim announced the cast: Marvel actor Simu Liu, Crazy Rich Asians star Constance Wu, comedian Iliza Shlesinger, and Ray Romano.

Also playing - all for charity - is Who's Line star Aisha Tyler, Patton Oswalt, Superbad's Michael Cera, The Office's B.J. Novak, and WNBA's Candace Parker.

Mayim stood tall in a white lab coat with a wide smile with her long hair down and signature specs - now the full look has been revealed hinting she's elevating her style for the celebs.

"It's the OG Jeopardy, with celebrities," she said in the clip, hilariously shading: "Let's hope they've been reading more than screenplays."

But on Facebook after the announcement, one slammed: "Am I the only one who has never heard of any of these celebrities? Just Patton Oswalt and Ray Romano."

Another agreed: "I only know Ray Romano, who are the rest?"

'MORE ARE COMING'

Luckily for critics, on the podcast Inside Jeopardy!, EP Mike Davies promised last month: "we more coming in on a daily basis.”

“It’s hard - the celebrities don’t take the test. We don’t interview them so we have to sort of divide who will be good at this game," he added on choosing the stars to play.

He also teased: "We are introducing a new format for Celebrity Jeopardy!. We’re testing it out, we will be announcing a new format [soon.]"

“We are gonna be tweaking the format... we will be announcing that in a few weeks.”

BIG SHOES TO FILL

Celebrity Jeopardy! first aired in 1992, and the last tournament took place in 2015.

The special has only previously been hosted by the late Alex Trebek, who passed away in 2020 from pancreatic cancer at age 80.

Since its debut, Celebrity Jeopardy! has featured over 200 celebrity contestants, including Anderson Cooper, Jodie Foster, Regis Philbin, Pat Sajak, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Stephen King, and so many other household names.

So famous was the spinoff that it scored the famed Saturday Night Live parody skits - some of Will Ferrell’s "most popular" sketches per The Hollywood Reporter (he of course played an over-it Alex.)

“I loved them,” Alex once said on the send-ups, per the outlet. “It means you’ve arrived."

So clearly Mayim has a lot to live up to for fans, but she looks dressed for success.

Celebrity Jeopardy! will air on Sundays at 8 pm ET on the ABC network and each episode will be available to stream on Hulu one day after it airs.
