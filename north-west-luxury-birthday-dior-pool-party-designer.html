Inside North Wests luxury 10th pink-themed birthday party in pictures - from Dior pool party to $

KIM Kardashian has thrown her daughter North an epic pool party in Beverly Hills to celebrate her 10th birthday.

North's friends were treated to a luxury sleepover, a day at the pool, and a trip to Dior's incredible pop-up boutique.

North shared a video of the pool at the Beverly Hills Hotel, decked out with pink sunloungers and umbrellas.

The preteen and her friends headed to a Dior store, which showcased shelves of necklaces, bracelets, and mini-totes.

It looked like North and several of her pals treated themselves to woven J'Adior bracelets, which are worth $420.

The group camped out the night before her birthday in a suite at a five-star hotel.

They slept on lilac teepee beds adorned with fairy lights, giant Hello Kitty cushions, and a basket of goodies.

The entrance to the suite was stacked with shelves full of brand-new pairs of sneakers, sweaters, and water bottles.

North shared footage of the food and drink that was on offer, including cans of Hello Kitty Fizzy Pop soda.

North and her friend Eva had their own personalized mocktails called North by NorthWest and Eva's Lavender Haze.

North's drink consisted of grape soda, pop rocks, and cotton candy, while Eva's was made with lemonade and blueberries.

SWEET TREATS

The group tucked into all sorts of sweet treats, including mini cakes with fruit on top, chocolates, candies, and fresh strawberries.

North had a birthday cake with some decorative macarons on top.

Multi-colored macarons were stacked on top of one another on a silver plate.

The delicious treats kept on coming when she showed off three icing-covered eclairs.

North and Eva also had a huge gray multi-tier cake, which guests crowded around while singing happy birthday.

ALL THE FAMILY

Kim's older sister Kourtney Kardashian could be seen singing in the background, while her 10-year-old daughter Penelope Disick stood next to Kim and sang using a microphone.

As the song came to an end, North and Eva blew out the candles and everyone applauded them.

North then shouted: "Wait, a speech!" before adding: "Happy birthday to us!"

Kim then turned to her niece Penelope and announced: "Oh P, sing it girl!" as the tween started belting out a rendition of the birthday song again.

The night before North's birthday, Kim took the preteen and her friends on a party bus to dinner at Nobu.

She also shared a video of her and North dancing alongside TikTok brothers NoLimitJay and Mias.

The group did the so-called wiggy dance the brothers made famous on TikTok during North's birthday party.
