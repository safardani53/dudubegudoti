IT can often be hard to find the perfect wire-free bra, especially for the curvier among us who need that little bit of extra support.

But you don't have to rely on under-wiring and padding, and there are some fabulous fashionable options out there for those with bigger busts.

Here, size 14-16 32G bust curve model and content creator Sophie Hughes, 32, from Cheshire, shares her five top non-wired bra choices she swears by.

Sophie says: "The perfect bra for me is a combination of supportive, comfy and sexy.

"Lingerie is supposed to fit you, it’s not something you squeeze your body in to, and it gives you so much confidence when it fits you well."

And, perhaps surprisingly, her favourite one is a supermarket purchase which she rates even better than Skims...

Primark

Sophie says: "I absolutely love these Primark cami-style bralettes.

"They're an affordable everyday go-to when I’m working from home or feel like having an extra comfy day!

"These colours are absolutely gorgeous for spring too, and the ribbed material adds an extra pop and makes them feel really stylish." 

To buy: Primark three pack of ribbed cropped cami bralettes, £11.

Skims

Sophie says: "Skims can do no wrong when it comes to the fit and quality of their lingerie.

"This red bralette is one I often wear around the house with matching joggers.

"It gives your boobs the perfect lift and the deep cut makes it super flattering.

"It's sexy in an understated way whilst also being really fashionable - love, love, love!"

To buy: Skims cotton plunge bralette, available in ten colours, £36.

Freya  

Sophie says: "For me this is a really beautiful take on a non-wired bra.

"It's soft, it's feminine and it's sexy whilst still giving you an element of support.

"The quality of all Freya bras is absolutely incredible too and this is one of my firm faves." 

To buy: Freya's Erin Bralette, available in five colours, £28.

Marks and Spencer 

Sophie says: "This is my favourite non-wired bra EVER.

"This is my favourite because it gives you the full support and lift of a traditional wired bra, but it's super comfy.

"You could wear this all day and night, fab for the gym, and fab under any outfit.

"I absolutely love this colour for spring/summer too and it's true to size."

To buy: Marks and Spencer non-wired full cup bra, available F-H, £22.

Pour Moi

Sophie says: "With a front fastening clasp this bra is the perfect mix of sexy and comfy.

"I absolutely love the colour of this as I think it's really different.

"It's supportive and I felt really beautiful in this, the clasp is also really different and much easier to put on than trying to squeeze a bralette overhead."

To buy: Pour Moi lace front fastening non-wired bralette, £36.

Boux Avenue

Sophie says: "I absolutely lived in this loungewear sporty style set last summer because it was so comfy and perfect under a t-shirt. 

"They don't have it in orange this season but the bright pink is still gorgeous!

"Everything Boux Avenue do is always incredible in quality and I will be buying this in every colour this season."

To buy: Boux Avenue ribbed seamless lounge bralette, available in three colours, £18.

Finally, whatever bra you choose, Sophie believes the most important thing is to feel comfortable and have a few secret-weapon wardrobe items that you always feel fabulous in, as that will make you feel extra confident.

Sophie adds: "One of my top tips is if you’re having a bad day and feel horrible in everything you try on, go back to an outfit you felt beautiful and confident in before.

"It’s not the clothes - it’s your mindset so to go back to an old faithful.

"I think dressing for your size and shape is really important and you should wear things which make you feel confident versus just what’s in fashion this week.
