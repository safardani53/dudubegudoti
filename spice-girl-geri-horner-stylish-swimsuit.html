Spice Girl Geri Horner looks incredible as she slips into stylish swimsuit for lake day with her kid

SPICE Girls singer Geri Horner showed off her incredible figure as she stripped down to a one-shoulder swimsuit on a family day out.

The performer was enjoying some down time with her kids as she headed to a lake in Austin, Texas.

Geri looked fantastic in the one-piece as she paraded her enviable figure for all to see.

She kept her famous ginger locks tied loosely at the back of her head as she enjoyed a dip in the water.

The family appeared to be looking for turtles as she captioned her set of Instagram photos: "Turtle adventure in Austin."

Geri, 51, is a mum-of-two with her daughter Bluebell being born in 2006 with her ex-partner, Sacha Gervasi.

She and husband Christian Horner welcomed a son to their clan in January 2017.

The Bag It Up singer's fans were left going wild over her svelte look in the comments section of her post.

One fan wrote to the star: "You look amazing x."

Another added: "Awesome photos Geri and I love them very much."
