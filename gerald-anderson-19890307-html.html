Gerald Anderson (TV Actor) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Gerald Randolph Opsima Anderson Jr. is an American Filipino actor. He is known to have starred in dramas such as Sana Maulit Muli, My Girl, an action television series, Tayong Dalawa in which he plays a soldier and NBI agent in Kung Tayo'y Magkakalayo, a mentally handicapped youth in Budoy, In the television adaptation of the iconic comic book character "Fredo" in Dyesebel by Mars Ravelo, the television series Fantasy, Nathaniel and in the director's film On The Job Festival de Cannes. Anderson won his first Best Actor award with the 24th PMPC Star Award for Television for an episode of Maalaala Mo Kaya, in which he played a person with Tourette syndrome. In December 2015, he received 21 acting nominations, he won 10 best actor awards and 1 supporting role award. He has been hailed twice as the “Prince of Filipino Film and Television” and “Favorite Lovers” along with his former co-star, Kim Chiu, at the Guillermo Mendoza Box Office Awards.
