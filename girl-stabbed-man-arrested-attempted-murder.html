Horror as girl, 15, is 'stabbed in street by man, 23, in Middlesbrough' - with 'attacker' charged wi

A MAN has been charged with attempted murder after a schoolgirl was stabbed in the street.

Emergency services scrambled to the scene after the attack and raced the girl, 15, to hospital.

She still reminds there today in a stable condition.

Police quickly arrested a man, 23, who they say knew her.

He has today been charged with attempted murder and remains in custody.

Emergency services were rushed to Premier Road, Middlesbrough, around 3pm on Thursday after the horror unfolded.

Cleveland Police say they are not looking for anybody further.

The force said: "Anyone who witnessed the incident, or who has any information or was in the area at the time and has not yet spoken to Cleveland Police, is asked to get in touch.

"Please dial 101 and speak to the Control Room, quoting reference number SE23121727.

"Alternatively, you can go to the Public Portal and provide information and submit any CCTV, video footage or dashcam footage. You can access the Public Portal here Public Portal (mipp.police.uk)"
