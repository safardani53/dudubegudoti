Who Is Logan Palmer? Facts About The Bachelorette Contestant Hollywood Life

  • Logan Palmer is a contestant on season 8 of ‘Bachelor in Paradise.’
  • He has been spending time with Shanae Ankney. They went on a one-on-one date and gave each other roses at the first two rose ceremonies.
  • Logan was on season 19 of ‘The Bachelorette,’ where he struggled to decide whether he wanted to pursue Rachel Recchia or Gabby Windey.

Logan Palmer has been going strong with Shanae Ankney on season 8 of Bachelor in Paradise so far. He gave her his rose at the first ceremony, and they also went on a one-on-one date. Shanae pursued James Bonsall during week two, but wound up giving Logan her rose at the second rose ceremony.

However, the relationship will be put to the test this week, as the couples were separated and put in two different locations on the Oct. 17 episode. It remains to be seen whether or not Shanae and Logan’s relationship can survive the split week. Learn more about Logan below.

1. What Happened To Logan On ‘The Bachelorette’?

Logan was on season 19 of The Bachelorette with Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia. During the first few weeks of filming, Logan couldn’t decide which of the women he wanted to pursue. He wound up accepting a rose from Rachel during the third rose ceremony, which was his way of committing to her. However, he quickly started realizing that his feelings for Gabby were stronger.

Logan didn’t get a chance to tell Rachel how he was feeling ahead of the next rose ceremony, and he went on to still accept another rose from her, which was very controversial on social media. The next week, he finally came clean, and then also opened up to Gabby about his feelings for her. She decided to give Logan a chance, and he got a rose from Gabby. However, Logan had to leave the show during week 6, as he tested positive for COVID-19.

2. What Is Logan’s Job?

Logan works as a videographer in San Diego, California. He has worked as a Production Technician at Raindrop since Sept. 2021, according to his LinkedIn. Before that, he was a camera operator for the San Diego Padres. He also worked in production and videography at KUSI Television prior to that.

3. Where Did Logan Go To School?

Logan graduated from Northern Arizona University in 2018. He has a Bachelor’s Degree in Strategic Communications, with an emphasis in Advertising.

4. Logan Loves The Beach

Logan loves spending time at the beach and is a huge fan of “the sun and surf,” according to his ABC bio. Sevreal of Logan’s Instagram posts are of him at the beach.

5. How Old Is Logan Palmer?

Logan is 26 years old. He was the same age as Rachel when they filmed The Bachelorette, and five years younger than Gabby.
