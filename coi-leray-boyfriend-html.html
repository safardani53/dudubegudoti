Coi Leray Tells Her Boyfriend Pressas Admirers to Back Off

Coi Leray and her boyfriend Pressa are reputed to be one of the hottest couples in the world of hip hop. 

However, after a few suspicious tweets from Leray, she was rumored to have broken up with her boyfriend a few weeks back. 

The rapper seemed to announce their separation with a now-deleted tweet when she wrote, "Single." 

As a result, she received a lot of raised eyebrows from her fans as they speculated she was talking about her relationship status.

She followed up another tweet a few minutes later by saying, "If your [expletive] lets you go to sleep mad every night, you need a new [expletive]."

However, a few days after she posted the now-deleted tweets, Leray shared a snap of Pressa carrying her on his shoulder with her 5.4 million followers on her Instagram.

She asked her followers to stream her boyfriend's music, making it quite apparent that the couple was still together. 

Coi Leray Showed Vigilance For Her Boyfriend Through Tweets

Leray is clearly obsessed with her boyfriend's gorgeous looks. She never misses a chance to compliment his beauty, as she has frequently pointed out how stunning he is.

Rapper Pressa, born to a Filipino mom and Jamaican dad, has a distinct look because of his biracial features. 

Leray showed she is protective of Pressa with her recent tweets. 

She wrote, "Yes, he's a pretty boy and has manners, but don't get it twisted!"

After her first tweet, she left yet another post on Twitter, where she told his admirers to stay away from him as the two of them are in love with each other. 

She wrote, "His beautiful brown skin and long Asian hair might tickle y'all feathers, but try it [expletive]."

The fellow rappers are speculated to have started dating during the production 'Attachments' in May 2021. 

Coi Leray Previously Dated Fellow Rapper Trippie Redd

Before she started dating Pressa, Leray was romantically involved with fellow rapper Trippie Redd back in 2019.

Redd admitted his relationship with Leray a few months after his public split with fellow rapper Ayleks. 

Leray caught Redd's attention through his manager Milo, and although she initially wasn't into the rapper, they met on tour and immediately clicked. 

The pair were vocal about their relationship as they frequently showed up in the public practicing PDA and shared adorable snaps with their followers on the Internet. 

Unfortunately, Coi Leray and Trippie Redd called it a quit after a few months of dating in 2020. 

During her interview with No Jumper in August 2020, Leray confessed she does not regret dating her ex-boyfriend Redd.

"At the end of the day, that's who I [expletive] dated, and it was public," she said. 

Before concluding the interview, she wished the best for her ex-boyfriend, calling him "a star" and "a big dude out here."
