Emma Watson’s Wardrobe Malfunction — Flashes SPANX At ‘Noah’ Premiere:

The svelte star was wearing a pair of shaping shorts — and although they weren’t meant to be seen we caught a glimpse of them as she walked down the carpet in her sensational dress. Emma looked fantastic, from head-to-toe, and the gorgeous gown was an amazing choice on the star. She finished off the look with a bold, tangy lip and a neat and chic updo, adding to the effortless glamour of the ensemble.

Sure, while your SPANX are meant to stay concealed underneath your clothes, we don’t think this minor wardrobe mishap is a big deal at all! Emma looked so good in the dress and really rocked the daring slit — and we can’t hold this little slip against her. In fact, we commend her for remaining properly covered up. Showing off some shaping shorts is far less embarrassing than flashing your privates!

Emma rarely ever has a hair out of place so it is a nice reminder that the stylish star sometimes suffers a minor mixup, just like everyone else. Do you agree?
