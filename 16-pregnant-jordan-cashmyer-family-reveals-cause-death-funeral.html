THE FAMILY of Jordan Cashmyer has revealed her cause of death and begged for financial help to pay for her funeral.

The 16 & Pregnant alum tragically passed away earlier this month at the age of 26.

The MTV star’s family revealed the cause of Jordan’s passing on a GoFundMe page, sharing that “addiction” was how she passed away.

The post, which was penned by Jordan’s father Dennis, read: “Our daughter Jordan tragically lost her life on January 15, 2022, at the age of 26.

“Leaving behind a sweet little 6-month-old girl.

“Addiction plagues many families, and our family was not immune to it.”

The post continued: “Jordan was always so full of life and full of personality.

“She had a way of always making those around her laugh and smile, and her smile was truly contagious.”

After saying the family is “heartbroken” and they “will forever miss Jordan,” the family asked for help.

They wrote: “We are now faced with the grim outcome that our granddaughter that we have been raising will never get to make memories with her mother or father, never getting to know them."

The family shared that in addition to losing Jordan, Lyla "lost her father to addiction at two months."

They continued: “Our family has to now not only put our daughter to rest but navigate through starting over with an infant who has lost both her parents in her six months of life.

“Please help our family cover the costs of Jordan's memorial and start a fund for Lyla's future.

“We did not foresee starting back over, and all that comes along with that.

“Lyla has been our family's greatest joy and addition; she has been our saving grace during this difficult time.”

The post concluded with the family saying they have received “so much love and words of kindness in the past nine days since Jordan's passing” and that they are "grateful."

TRAGIC PASSING

A medical examiner confirmed the former 16 & Pregnant star's death to TMZ earlier this month.

Dennis reflected on his daughter's passing in a Facebook post the morning after her death.

Alongside a black and white photo of Jordan, he wrote: "Last night I received a call no parent ever wants.My oldest beloved daughter, Jordan has passed away, she was only 26 years old.

"Our hearts are truly broken. No parent should ever have to go through losing a child, EVER."

He continued: "Please keep my family in your thoughts prayers as we navigate through this terrible tragedy.

"Please understand and respect our privacy at this time.

"We are surrounded by the love and support of our children, grand daughter Lyla and family members."

REALITY TV PAST

Jordan, her boyfriend, Derek Taylor, and daughter Evie appeared on MTV's 16 & Pregnant back in 2014.

The young parents' heartbreaking story played out on the show, as they were homeless while Jordan prepared to give birth.

Her family did not approve of her relationship with Derek, which led to them essentially disowning the then-pregnant teen.

While the pair did not go on to continue telling their story on one of the franchise spinoffs, they continued to struggle as they raised their daughter.

Jordan and Derek split following their appearance on 16 & Pregnant, while they were not able to properly care for their young daughter.  

Jordan's dad and stepmom stepped in and temporarily shared guardianship of their granddaughter.

The young mother eventually signed away her custody rights to Derek's mom on a temporary basis in 2015.  

ADDICTION STRUGGLE

According to TMZ, she was struggling with mental health issues, addiction, and had suicidal thoughts at the time.

Jordan later dealt with legal issues, while she was working at a strip club and got arrested for drug possession by 2017.
