Shailene Woodley Hollywood Life

Bio:
Shailene Woodley (born Shailene Diann Woodley on November 15, 1991 in Simi Valley, California) is an American actress. Though she’s most famous for starring on ABC Family’s The Secret Life of the American Teenager, Shailene received the most critical praise for playing George Clooney‘s daughter in 2011’s The Descendants . She also got America’s attention when she snagged leading roles in The Amazing Spider-Man 2 and the big-screen adaptation of John Green‘s novel The Fault In Our Stars.
